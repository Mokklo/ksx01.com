About me

I’m a graduate student majoring in Economics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interests draw on labor economics and quantitative methods.

Recently, I’ve been exploring the effect of machine learning in fitting proxy variables for economic level, and trying to transform pool data into panel data using a new estimation method.
